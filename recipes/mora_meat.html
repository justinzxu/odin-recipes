Mora Meat

Description

It's a pocket of dough filled with meat. Image of mora is stamped on the pastry. Revives character and restore 100 hp.

Ingredients

  • 1 Raw Meat
  • 1 Flour

Steps

  1. Select Ningguan
  2. Add 1 Raw Meat
  3. Add 1 Flour
  4. Ace the cooking minigame
  5. Pray that you receive Qiankun Mora Meat instead
