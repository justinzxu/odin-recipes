Jade Parcel

Description

Ham and veggies tied in a bag of cabbage leaves, it's got broth too!

Ingredients

  • 3 Lotus heads
  • 2 Jueyun Chili
  • 2 Cabbage
  • 1 Ham

Steps

  1. Throw all those veggies in the pot
  2. Put in the ham
  3. Add chili to spice it up
  4. Win the cooking minigame!

Special Dish
